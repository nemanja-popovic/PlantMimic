How does the PlantMimic work

PlantMimic platform is cloud solution that helps your organization with many important processes. It helps you to

Define schemas

Now it is easier than ever to upload your plant schema and start using all platform features. You can easily add, edit, delete and search over all your plant schemas.

Track processes and signals

With just a few simple clicks you can start tracking signals in all parts of your plant schema. You can also specify minimal and maximal accepted value for every signal.

Have realtime alerts

If signal is not within allowed and defined values you get notified immediately.
